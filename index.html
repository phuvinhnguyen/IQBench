Welcome to IQBench

A Benchmark for Evaluating Fluid Intelligence of Vision-Language Models (VLMs) using standardized visual IQ tests.

What is IQBench?

  • 500 human-curated questions
  • 10 reasoning domains
  • Emphasis on both prediction accuracy and interpretability of reasoning

For more information, please refer to our blog post.
